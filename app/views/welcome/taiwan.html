Audition in Taiwan

Ballet dancers from Taiwan travel to Bolshoi Ballet Academy.

Four dancers were chosen to attend Bolshoi Ballet Academy Summer Intensive in 2013. Two dancers were invited to stay and join the yearly program at the Bolshoi Academy in Moscow.
